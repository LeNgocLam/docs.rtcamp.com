Introduction

rtMedia PRO Settings

rtMedia PRO add-on, provides several additional settings and options over the rtMedia Free version. This page covers only the additional settings provided with rtMedia PRO version. (Check Documentation for rtMedia Core Settings)

You will see the following tabs when you click the rtMedia link on your Dashboard.

  1. Display
  1. WordPress
  1. bbPress
  1. BuddyPress
  1. Default Albums
  1. Types

  1. Image Sizes

  2. Sidebar Widgets

  1. Privacy
  1. Custom CSS
  1. Reward Points
  1. Moderation
  1. Other Settings
