## Sidebar widgets: Galleries

This addon provide widgets to upload media and display gallery for rtMedia plugin. You can use the widgets in site sidebar area where users can upload a media files. Also the sidebar widget can display uploaded media like recent media, most viewed media, popular media, etc.
